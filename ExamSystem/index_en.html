Welcome to Vavelya Academy – Your Modern Online Examination System

“Every student has a voice. Let your knowledge speak.”


Vavelya Academy is a powerful and user-friendly online examination platform designed for both teachers and students. Our system makes creating, managing, and taking exams simple, fast, and secure.


Whether you are a teacher who wants to evaluate students efficiently or a student who wants to test knowledge and track progress, Vavelya Academy is built for you.



Explore Subjects

Our platform supports multiple subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • Informatics
  • Literature
  • Music

Each subject contains organized units, allowing students to practice specific topics step by step.

Use the search feature to quickly find questions, exams, or units.



Create Your Own Exam

Teachers can easily create customized exams:

  • Add questions
  • Add multiple answers
  • Mark correct answers
  • Generate a unique Exam ID
  • Share the Exam ID with students

The system automatically calculates results and stores them securely.



For Teachers

Teachers get access to a personal dashboard with:

  • A table showing all students
  • Individual student results
  • Exam scores
  • Performance statistics
  • Progress tracking by unit and subject

This helps teachers analyze strengths and weaknesses quickly.



For Students

  • Join exams using Exam ID
  • View their results instantly
  • Track their performance
  • See previous exam history in a results table

Students can monitor their improvement over time.



How It Works

We provide:

  • Explanation videos
  • Step-by-step tutorial images
  • Guides for creating exams
  • Instructions for joining exams

Even first-time users can easily understand the system.



Smart Results System

All results are displayed in clear and simple tables:

  • Teachers see all student results
  • Students see only their personal results

The system ensures privacy and secure data management.
